Enumclaw,

There is something magical about this town - something only a lucky few of us get to partake in every day.

Waking up to fertile farmlands surrounded by towering evergreen trees - framed perfectly by our previous rolling foothills and as always kept watch by the awe-inspiring, snow covered Rainier. The smell of a left over rainstorm mixed with fresh fertilized fields can only be admired by us locals. Enumclaw is more than a place to live, it's a place where people live together. A town where grocery shopping is the same as social hour, where following a tractor is part of the daily commute, and a place where kids can still ride on their bikes downtown. I love Enumclaw. I love knowing the names of our police officers, waiters, store checkers, I love the fact that life here runs just a little bit slower. We take our time â€“ we focus on our community. Enumclaw is a town strife with strong values, morals unique character. Good ol' hardworking people who are waiting with helping hands. There is a reason why very few leave this town and why most of them tend to come back. There's something magical about this place and I'm proud I get to spend my life here in the Claw.
